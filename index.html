Welcome

Practicing the art of dentistry since 1986, "Dr. Bob" (Robert P. Cox, DDS) provides the finest quality dental care available today. Dr. Bob's experience in general and cosmetic dentistry includes crowns, bridges, porcelain veneers, bonding, implant restorations, dentures, partials, extractions, silver and tooth-colored fillings, and teeth whitening. Dr. Bob and his staff provide a quality of dentistry to create the winning smile you so desire. Through his frequent participation at continuing education courses, Dr. Bob ensures that his patients always receive the latest, most effective treatments possible that they can afford.
