Dental Services

Our treatment philosophy is geared toward preventing future dental problems. We treat patients of most ages and we offer a broad range of treatment modalities, ranging from the most basic to the state of the art. Our comprehensive services include the following:


Dr. Bob's Smile Shop performs the following procedures

  • Preventive Dentistry
  • Dental Cleaning
  • Direct and Indirect Restorations (fillings)
  • Crowns and Bridges
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Cosmetic Bonding
  • Sealants
  • Tooth Whitening
  • Extractions
  • Implant Restoration

    • We refer the following procedures to outside specialists

    • Periodontal (gum) problems are referred out to specialists
    • Impacted Tooth Extractions are referred out to specialists
    • Implant Placement is referred out to specialists
    • Root Canal Therapy is referred out to specialists
    • Young Children MAY be referred out to specialists

    We understand the importance of providing these services to our patients in the most comfortable, stress-free manner. Our goal is to provide painless procedures with local anesthesia as necessary.
